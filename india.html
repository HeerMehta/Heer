Corona in India It's effect in India

India currently has 593 reported active cases of the novel coronavirus, the Union Health and Family Welfare Ministry has said it its latest update.
The total number of reported cases in India is 649. This comprises 602 Indians and 47 foreign nationals. Of these, 47 have recovered. However, the death toll now stands at 13. The highest number of cases are in Maharastra with over 100 confirmed case.
The states like Gujarat, Rajasthan, Haryana, Punjab etc are lesser affected and states of Madhya Pradesh, Orissa , Chhatisgarh the least. Also, some of the north eastern states and parts of Jammu and Kashmir remain untouched by the virus as on 26 th March, 2020.
