Corona in United States Thousands killed in industrial superpower

Since the first U.S. case of the new coronavirus was reported on Jan. 20 in Washington state, 65,434 people have been diagnosed, with clusters around Seattle, New York City and in California. At least 937 people have died. American health officials have identified multiple people with Covid-19, as the infection is called, without known ties to other outbreaks or patients—a sign the virus is transmitting person-to-person in some places. A lack of testing has hamstrung health workers’ efforts to track the infection.
